WHAT WE DO

YOOOART AIMS TO SUPPORT ARTISTS NOT YET RECOGNIZED ON AN INTERNATIONAL LEVEL, PARTICULARLY IN FRANCE, EAST ASIA, AND ENGLISH SPEAKING COUNTRIES.

We imagine projects and give artists the opportunity to take part in alone or in collaboration with others.

AT YOOOART, WE BELIEVE INTERDISCIPLINARY AND INTERCULTURAL EXCHANGE IS THE FOUNDATION OF ARTISTIC CREATION.

After the completion of a project, we work hard to ensure exposure of their work to galleries, competitions, festivals or other artistic platforms. Every month, new talented artist is featured on our website. We also occasionally organize or co-organize events between artists and the public (workshops, parties, conferences).
